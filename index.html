Expressions For Us

Greeting Cards for the ones you Love

Tired of searching endlessly for a wedding card for a gay or lesbian couple, or looking for an anniversary card for your wife or husband or any occasions for that matter. I was too, I had been making my wife cards for about five years now and then in 2012 decided well maybe I could design cards for others to..... I launched Expressions for Us in 2013 online and didn't really have much success, but I never let go of the dream of designing greeting cards for gay and lesbians. I just kept feeling the passion that there was a market for cards for same sex couples. So after some encouragement from my wife and some friends I opened an Etsy Store in spring of 2016 and I have started realizing that there are people out there interested in my cards. Coming soon I will have my cards on my website available for purchase.
