Expressions For Us

Greeting Cards for the ones you Love

Tired of searching endlessly for a wedding card for a gay or lesbian couple, or looking for an anniversary card for your wife or husband or any occasions for that matter. I was too.

I had been making my wife cards for about five years now and then in 2012 decided well maybe I could design cards for others to. I launched Expressions for Us in 2013 online and didn't really have much success, but I never let go of the dream of designing greeting cards for gay and lesbians. I just kept feeling the passion that there was a market for cards for same sex couples.

After some encouragement from my wife and friends, I opened an Etsy Store in spring of 2016 and I have started realizing that there are people out there interested in my cards.

Customized Cards!

Cards can be customized with your own photo, dates, and more to make them even more special! Simply indicate the customization in the notes field when ordering, or email me.

  • Cards are printed on heavyweight 100# cover card stock
  • Each card is cut, scored and packaged by me in my home studio.
  • All my cards have my logo on the back.
