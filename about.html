Expressions For Us

Greeting Cards for the ones you Love

I started this idea of designing greeting cards for same sex couples about five years ago. I am a person who loves to give cards....just because, and of course for those special occasions. However, I quickly realized there was nothing available in the stores and at that time not much was available online either. With that being said, the wheels started turning......and haven't stopped since!

So I started making my own cards for my wife, and Expressions For Us was born and the journey began. I hope you enjoy!
